Personal Profile

The Myer Briggs Test showed that I am encouraging and enjoy socialising. This means that in a group I can encourage other team members to not feel down and help them out in doing their work by talking to them.

It also stated that I am observant and entertaining. In a group I would first observe the way my team members act and when theirs a difficult situation or when everyone's tired I could be the one to cheer them up to keep going.

The test mentioned that I would provide emotional support so whenever a personal conflict clashes with their work, I could give emotional support and help them out. The downsides the test described were that I am a poor planner and not independent, which means I would rely on others and be forgetful at times.

The data shows that I am emotionally stable, friendly and optimistic. With these aspects my team members can rely on me with anything and I can lift everyone's mood up. It says I'm not really talkative so in the group there might be some miscommunication due to the lack of talking. It also says I'm disorganized, which could be catastrophic when all the work is put together.

The Learning Styles Test showed that I was 40% Auditory. It stated that I learn best by hearing and listening, and that I remember and understand things I've heard. I store information by the way it sounds and I easily understand spoken instructions. In a group I would be able to follow instructions from team members giving instructions through speech rather than online and I would be able to work efficiently by discussing the project with the members in my group.
